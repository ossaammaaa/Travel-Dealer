About New York City

New York City is the largest city in the United States. It is one of the world’s great centers of culture and business. Its fast pace and constant activity earned it the nickname “the city that never sleeps.” New York City is located in the state of New York. It lies where the Hudson and East rivers join New York Bay. The city is made up of five districts called boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, the Bronx, Queens, and Staten Island.

Landmarks

Explore the iconic landmarks of New York City. Visit the One World Observatory at the World Trade Center for breathtaking views of the city, take a ferry to Liberty Island to see the Statue of Liberty, and experience the dazzling lights of Broadway in Times Square.

Restaurants

Indulge in the diverse culinary scene of NYC. Enjoy an unforgettable dining experience at Per Se where French cuisine meets perfection. Immerse yourself in the flavors of Italy at Il Cortile in Little Italy, and elevate your dining experience at Top of the Rock Restaurant with both delicious meals and panoramic views.

Hotels

Experience luxury at renowned hotels in NYC. Stay at the iconic The Plaza Hotel near Central Park, offering timeless elegance. Enjoy modern comforts at The Standard, High Line in the trendy Meatpacking District, or opt for a charming boutique experience at The Bowery Hotel in Lower Manhattan.
