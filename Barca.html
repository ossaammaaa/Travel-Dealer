Welcome to Barcelona

About Barcelona

Barcelona, city, seaport, and capital of Barcelona provincia (province) and of Catalonia comunidad autónoma (autonomous community), northeastern Spain, located 90 miles (150 km) south of the French border. It is Spain’s major Mediterranean port and commercial centre and is famed for its individuality, cultural interest, and physical beauty.

Landmarks

Explore the breathtaking Sagrada Família, a masterpiece by Antoni Gaudí. Stroll through the colorful and whimsical Park Güell and immerse yourself in the Gothic architecture of the Gothic Quarter.

Restaurants

Indulge in the rich flavors of Catalan cuisine at renowned restaurants. Experience traditional dishes like seafood paella at Xiringuito Escribà, enjoy tapas at Cervecería Moritz, and savor local specialties at Cinc Sentits.

Hotels

Experience luxury at Hotel Majestic with stunning views of Passeig de Gràcia. For a boutique stay, consider Hotel Neri in the heart of the Gothic Quarter. Budget-friendly options include Hotel Curious near La Rambla.
